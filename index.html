Events and ticketing

"BigShareef excels in seamless event management and ticketing solutions, ensuring memorable experiences and smooth entry for every attendee."

  • Online Ticketing Platform:

    BigShareef provides a user-friendly online ticketing platform, allowing event organizers to sell tickets, manage registrations, and provide easy access for attendees.

  • Event Management Software:

    A comprehensive event management software solution by BigShareef helps organizers plan, coordinate, and execute events seamlessly, including features for scheduling, attendee management, and logistics.

  • Customized Event Packages:

    BigShareef offers a service to create customized event packages, allowing organizers to tailor offerings such as VIP access, special seating, and promotional bundles to meet the unique needs of their events.

  • Branding and Promotional Materials:

    BigShareef provides branding and promotional material services, including the design and production of banners, posters, badges, and other branded items to enhance the visibility and recognition of the event.

  • Access Control Systems:

    BigShareef offers secure access control systems for events, utilizing technologies like QR codes or RFID wristbands to manage attendee access, monitor entry points, and enhance overall event security.

  • Post-Event Analytics and Reporting:

    BigShareef delivers post-event analytics services, providing organizers with detailed insights into attendee behavior, ticket sales, and overall event performance, enabling data-driven decisions for future events.
